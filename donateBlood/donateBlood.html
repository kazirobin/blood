Why Donate Blood

Your small act can give someone a second chance at life

Nepal needs over 5,000 donations every week.

Regular weekly donations ensure blood available for are need.

The gift of blood can only be given from one person to another, and there is no substitute. So it's nice to know Nepal can rely on everyday heroes' generosity to keep the flow of blood and plasma going.

However, the need for blood and plasma is constant, so we're looking for more lifesavers. By putting aside a small part of your day you can make a life-changing difference, you can become a lifesaver too!

Reasons to donate

  • One blood donation can save up to 3 lives.
  • Make a difference in your community by helping others.
  • Pay it forward. One day whanau, friends or even you may need it.
  • Join an extraordinary group of lifesavers.
  • Donating regularly ensures that a safe and plentiful supply of blood is available whenever and wherever it is needed.
  • Less than 4% of the eligible population in New Zealand are blood donors.
