About Us

Join the Lifesaving Mission

Who We Are

Our platform makes blood donation and management simple and efficient. Users can easily request blood or register as donors. When a requested blood type is available, the request is approved immediately, and the requester receives the required blood. If the blood type is not available, our system automatically sends an alert saying, “Requested blood is not available — please wait while we search for donors.” At the same time, an alert is sent to all previous donors of that blood group. Once a donor donates and the blood is added to our blood bank, the requester instantly receives an email or SMS notification that the requested blood type is now available. This ensures faster response, better communication, and timely help during emergencies.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make the blood donation and receiving process faster, easier, and more efficient. We strive to build trust among donors and recipients through transparency and reliability. Our goal is to inspire and motivate people in our society to donate blood regularly and save lives. Beyond just connecting donors and recipients, we aim to build a compassionate community — a family united by the spirit of giving and humanity.

Our Vision

To create a world where blood donation is seamless, reliable, and life-saving, ensuring that no one suffers due to the unavailability of blood. We envision a connected community where technology and compassion work together to deliver timely help during emergencies.

Why Donate Blood

Your small act can give someone a second chance at life

Nepal needs over 5,000 donations every week.

Regular weekly donations ensure blood available for are need.

The gift of blood can only be given from one person to another, and there is no substitute. So it's nice to know Nepal can rely on everyday heroes' generosity to keep the flow of blood and plasma going.

However, the need for blood and plasma is constant, so we're looking for more lifesavers. By putting aside a small part of your day you can make a life-changing difference, you can become a lifesaver too!

Reasons to donate

  • One blood donation can save up to 3 lives.
  • Make a difference in your community by helping others.
  • Pay it forward. One day whanau, friends or even you may need it.
  • Join an extraordinary group of lifesavers.
  • Donating regularly ensures that a safe and plentiful supply of blood is available whenever and wherever it is needed.
  • Less than 4% of the eligible population in New Zealand are blood donors.

Who needs your donation

Whether you choose to give blood your donation will save and improve the lives of people across Nepal.
